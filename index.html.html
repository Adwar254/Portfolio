Wind-Powered EV Charging System

Source: Chadlabs Scholarly Hub

The Problem: Addressing the challenge of 'range anxiety' in electric vehicles by developing a decentralized, renewable charging solution independent of the main power grid.

My Role & Methodology:
  • Researched aerodynamic principles and turbine efficiency using AI to synthesize academic papers.
  • Developed mathematical models to simulate energy output based on variable wind speeds.
  • Collaborated with a team to plan for future prototype construction and testing.

Outcome: The research resulted in a viable prototype concept with performance models indicating high potential for off-grid charging stations.
